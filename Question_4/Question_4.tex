\section{Brief discussion}\label{brief-discussion}}

It is hard to quantify quality and profitability from internet rankings,
but it does give us an indication of what is popular. To enter the
streaming market, audience scores are extremely important.

\hypertarget{analytics}{%
\section{Analytics}\label{analytics}}

\begin{verbatim}
## 75% 50% 25% 
## 7.1 6.4 5.7
\end{verbatim}

\begin{verbatim}
##   75%   50%   25% 
## 57549  9159  1793
\end{verbatim}

Based on this, movies can be split into four quartiles along popularity
and quality. Ideally, a streaming service would want films that are at
least in the top quartile for one metric and in the top two quartiles
for the other.
